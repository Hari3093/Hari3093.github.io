Britannia Industries Ltd - One Page Profile

Britannia Industries Limited is an Indian multinational food products company, which sells biscuits, breads and dairy products. Founded in 1892, it is one of India's oldest existing companies and currently part of the Wadia Group headed by Nusli Wadia.

About Britannia Industries

As of 2023, about 80% of revenues came from biscuit products. Britannia is one of the leading players in the business segment with a dominant market share (90% of revenue) in the Indian biscuit segment. The company has a wide range of biscuit portfolio across various categories including glucose, Marie, cookies, crackers, cream, milk, and health segments.

Strategic Initiatives & Growth Drivers

Capex - Mega Project in Ranjangaon, Maharashtra

Britannia Industries is building its mega project in Ranjangaon, Maharashtra to increase the capacity of all categories ranging from biscuits, cake, rusk, croissant & dairy plant. The company has invested ‚Çπ700 Cr so far and plans to add another ‚Çπ800 Cr strategically. The company will receive 110% incentive from the Maharashtra government after the completion of the project.

80:20 Growth Strategy

The company performed well during the COVID crisis by following the 80:20 rule. The company focused on 20% of the brands like Marie Gold, Good Day, Milk Bikis & NutriChoice, which contribute 80% of the company's revenue. These were put on priority list which enabled the company to streamline its productivity and increase the efficiency of production.

Dairy Expansion

The company sells cheese, beverages, milk and yoghurt under the dairy division, which directly reaches 100,000 outlets. The segment witnessed the highest growth, especially in cheese products which grew 300% during the year. The company is planning to start 5 new facilities and expand its Orissa plant by procuring 25,000 Litre/day from 1,000 farmers in Maharashtra.
